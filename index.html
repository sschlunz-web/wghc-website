Your first hike

How it works.

📅

1. Pick a hike

Browse the schedule. Each hike lists the meeting spot, leader, distance, and difficulty level.

⏰

2. Arrive 10 min early

Meet the hike leader at the start. No RSVP needed for guests — just show up.

💵

3. Share the cost

Any costs (parking, shuttle gas) are split evenly among everyone there. Most hikes are free.
