Our story

A hundred years of hikers, and counting.

Established in 1924, the Wisconsin Go Hiking Club exists to promote health and good fellowship by hiking. We're an all-volunteer community — no paid staff, no offices, just people who love getting outside together.

Mission

Health, fellowship, and Wisconsin trails.

Our purpose is simple: get people walking together. Hiking is what we do; the friendships are the reason we keep doing it.

The club focuses primarily on hiking, with occasional camping trips and social events. Everything is organized by volunteer trip leaders. Hikes typically run 2–8 miles at varying difficulty — from leisurely Ramblers on paved paths to Level 4 bushwhacks through mud and steep hills.

Guests are always welcome. Try a hike or two before deciding whether to join. Any costs are shared equally among the hikers — most outings are free, and the rest are very inexpensive.

Difficulty system

From Rambler to Level 4.

Every hike on the schedule is rated so you know what you're signing up for.

Rambler

Leisurely pace, plenty of stops. Great for new hikers and families.

Level 1

Paved paths or flat dirt trails. Comfortable for nearly anyone.

Level 2

Moderate pace, mild hills, possibly uneven footing.

Level 3

Brisk pace, longer distances, real elevation. Trail-fit hikers.

Level 4

Bushwhacking, mud, steep hills. Experienced hikers only.

Our history

A timeline.

1924

The club is founded

Wisconsin Go Hiking Club is established to promote health and good fellowship by hiking.

1958

The Kettle Moraine project

Members help survey what will become the Ice Age Trail's Kettle Moraine segments.

1980

National Scenic Trails era

Congress designates the Ice Age Trail and North Country Trail. WGHC trip leaders walk every Wisconsin mile the next year.

2024

Centennial year

One hundred years of hikes. The club is still growing, still volunteer-run.

2026

The digital Trail Finder

We launch a searchable catalog of every Ice Age, NCT, and local trail segment in Wisconsin — with maps and elevation profiles.
