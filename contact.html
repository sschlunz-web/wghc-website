FAQ

Common questions.

Do I need to be a member to join a hike?

No. Your first hike is always free. We just ask that you sign up for membership before your fourth visit.

What should I bring?

Sturdy shoes, water (a liter per two miles), a snack, and weather-appropriate layers. The trip leader will note anything extra in the hike description.

Are dogs welcome?

On most hikes, yes — leashed. A few segments cross sensitive habitat where dogs are not permitted; the schedule notes which.

Are hikes accessible?

Many trails on our finder are flat, paved, or boardwalk. Filter by "Easy" difficulty in the Trail Finder, or contact us — we'll find a good fit.

Can I lead a hike on a trail I know?

Yes — after attending three group hikes and completing our short leadership orientation. We love guest leaders.

How do you choose hikes?

Trip leaders propose hikes monthly. Our Outings Coordinator balances geography, difficulty, and season. The Trail Finder feeds many ideas.
