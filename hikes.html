What do the difficulty levels mean? ›
Rambler

Leisurely pace, frequent stops. Great for new hikers and families.

Level 1

Paved paths or flat dirt. Comfortable for almost anyone.

Level 2

Moderate pace, mild hills, possibly uneven footing.

Level 3

Brisk pace, longer distances, real elevation. Trail-fit hikers.

Level 4

Bushwhacking, mud, steep hills. Experienced hikers only.

Good to know

Before you go.

⏰

Arrive 10 min early

Find the leader, sign in as a guest if it's your first time, and we'll get rolling on time.

💵

Costs are shared

Any parking, shuttle, or carpool gas is split evenly among everyone on the hike.

🛡️

Hike at your own pace

Each hiker is responsible for their own safety. Bring water, layers, and good footwear.

📞

Cancellations

Leaders post cancellations as early as they can. When in doubt, contact the leader directly.
