How to join

Three simple steps.

1️⃣

Come on a hike

Pick one from the schedule. Arrive 10 min early and find the leader — no sign-up needed.

2️⃣

See if it's a fit

Walk with us a few times. Meet the regulars. Get a feel for our pace and culture.

3️⃣

Sign up & pay dues

Submit the form below, then pay annual dues online via our secure payment portal.

Member benefits

Why join.

📅

Advance hike details

Members get full hike details (meet location, leader contact) before they're public.

🚗

Carpool board

Members-only carpool listings for longer-distance hikes and shuttle-required routes.

🥾

Hike-leader eligible

After a few hikes, you can lead one yourself. We're always looking for new trip leaders.

🗳️

Vote in club business

Annual meeting, board elections, and the direction the club takes — members decide.
