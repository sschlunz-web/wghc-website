Guests are always welcome. Come on a hike or two — no fee, no commitment. If you love it, fill out the membership form below.

How to join

Three simple steps.

1️⃣

Come on a hike

Pick one from the schedule. Arrive 10 min early and find the leader — no sign-up needed.

2️⃣

See if it's a fit

Walk with us a few times. Meet the regulars. Get a feel for our pace and culture.

3️⃣

Fill out the form

Ready to join? Complete the membership form below. Annual dues keep the club running.

Member benefits

Why join.

📅

Advance hike details

Members get full hike details (meet location, leader contact) before they're public.

🚗

Carpool board

Members-only carpool listings for longer-distance hikes and shuttle-required routes.

🥾

Hike-leader eligible

After a few hikes, you can lead one yourself. We're always looking for new trip leaders.

🗳️

Vote in club business

Annual meeting, board elections, and the direction the club takes — members decide.

Membership form

Apply to join.

Fill out the form and we'll email you with payment instructions. Annual dues run on a 12-month rolling basis.

Cost-sharing model

Most hikes are free. When there are costs (parking, shuttle gas, group lodging on camping trips), they're divided evenly among everyone there.

Scholarships available

Cost is never a reason to stay home. Mention it in the notes and we'll waive dues.
